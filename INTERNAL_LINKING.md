# My Chennai Catering - Internal Linking Strategy

## Objective
Create a logical internal linking structure that improves crawlability, distributes page authority, and helps users navigate the website.

---

# 1. Site Hierarchy

Home
├── About
├── Services
│   ├── Wedding Catering
│   ├── Corporate Catering
│   ├── Birthday Catering
│   ├── Reception Catering
│   ├── Housewarming Catering
│   ├── Veg Catering
│   ├── Non-Veg Catering
│   ├── Buffet Catering
│   └── Live Food Counters
├── Menu
├── Gallery
├── Testimonials
├── Blog
└── Contact

---

# 2. Homepage Links

Link to:
- All service pages
- Gallery
- Testimonials
- Contact
- Latest blog posts

---

# 3. Service Page Links

Every service page should link to:
- Home
- Contact
- Gallery
- Testimonials
- Related Services
- Relevant Blog Articles

Example:
Wedding Catering →
• Reception Catering
• Veg Catering
• Live Food Counters
• Wedding Menu Guide (Blog)

---

# 4. Blog Linking Strategy

Each blog should include:
- 2–3 links to relevant service pages
- 1 link to Contact
- 1 link to Gallery

Example:
Blog: Wedding Catering Cost
Links:
- Wedding Catering
- Buffet Catering
- Contact

---

# 5. Navigation Links

Primary Navigation:
- Home
- About
- Services
- Menu
- Gallery
- Testimonials
- Blog
- Contact

---

# 6. Footer Links

Include:
- All Services
- About
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms & Conditions
- Google Business Profile
- Social Media

---

# 7. Breadcrumb Structure

Home > Services > Wedding Catering

Home > Blog > Wedding Catering Cost

Apply BreadcrumbList schema on every page.

---

# 8. Anchor Text Guidelines

Use descriptive anchor text:
✔ Wedding Catering Services in Chennai
✔ Corporate Catering Packages
✔ Birthday Catering Menus

Avoid:
✘ Click Here
✘ Read More

# 10. AI Implementation Rules

- No orphan pages
- Maximum 3 clicks from Home to any page
- Link related pages naturally
- Use keyword-rich anchor text
- Keep navigation consistent
- Add breadcrumbs to all pages
